About Us

Welcome to Artek Surfin Chemicals

Artek Surfin Chemicals Ltd. has been an ISO-certified company since 1998, specializing in surface finishing processes. The company provides a comprehensive range of products and services, including general metal finishing, electro-coloring, precious platings, and multi-layer PCB platings. Their user- and environmentally-friendly, consistently economical solutions have contributed to a group turnover exceeding 3000 crores. This success is attributed to their unwavering commitment to research and development, along with various global alliances, which enable them to offer state-of-the-art equipment and plating plants to the end-user industry.
